The Story Behind Da Big Homie

Rooted in Hip-Hop, Rising from the Diaspora

Our Roots

Da Big Homie began as a living tribute to the heartbeat of hip-hop culture and the vast creativity of the African diaspora. Conceived in late 2023 by a tight-knit group of local MCs, dancers, DJs, visual artists, and community organizers, our platform set out to be more than a music stream or an event list—it set out to be a digital griot, telling the story of our people’s resilience and unbreakable spirit.

Drawing deep from African oral traditions of storytelling, rhythm, and dance while honoring hip-hop’s Bronx block-party origins, Da Big Homie carved a space to celebrate “homegrown greatness” across every artistic discipline.

Our Mission

At Da Big Homie, we believe in three pillars:

  • Elevate
  • Empower
  • Unite
    • .

Our goal is to uplift underrepresented voices and leverage technology to foster real-world connections rooted in African diasporic traditions.

Join the Movement

Whether you’re an aspiring beat-maker in Accra, a graffiti artist in London, or a spoken-word poet in Atlanta, Da Big Homie is here to champion your voice. We need your ideas, your art, and your passion. Submit your work, pitch an event, or simply share your story—because every voice matters.
